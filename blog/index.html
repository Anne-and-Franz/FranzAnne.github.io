Lodging
Hotel blocks will be reserved at the Courtyard Marriott in Lebanon, NH-- Also check out alternative options if interested: Follansbee Inn,
Maple Hill Farm, or New London Inn.
Groups or Families
Feel free to rent a room or house with friends or family for the weekend near Lake Sunapee!
Activities
Please remember to bring swim clothes, towels and alternative shoes if you want to enjoy the lake!
There is a shallow beach for children and dock space for deep water swimming.
Other activities can include croquet, frisbee, badminton!
Travel
The Dartmouth Coach travels from both the Boston Logan Airport and South Station Train Station multiple times daily this bus can drop you off at the New London Park and Ride (very close to the reception area) as well as in Hanover, NH.
